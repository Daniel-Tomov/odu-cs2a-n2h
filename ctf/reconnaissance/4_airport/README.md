# Reconnaissance 4 - Airport
## Introduction
This challenge is from the National Cyber League Fall 2023 season. The challenge had two images of different airports.

[airport.jpg](airport.jpg) is a picture at gate A5/B3 at an unknown airport. There is a "RyanAir" plane in the background. 

[arrival.jpg](arrival.jpg) is a picture of the destination of the plane from the previous picture.

## Challenges
### Challenge 1
What is the airport code of the airport where [airport.jpg](airport.jpg) is taken?

### Challenge 2
What date was [airport.jpg](airport.jpg) taken?

### Challenge 3
What is the airport code of the airport in [arrival.jpg](arrival.jpg)?
