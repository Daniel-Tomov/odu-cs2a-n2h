## Example 2
Challenges can also be linked together. In other words, you'll need to solve the first one to help with the second to help with the third and so on. The following is from an actual CTF (with some modifications).

### The first challenge
>Find the Vietnam veteran who died on 05/11/1968 and lived in Virginia. What is his full name?
<details>
	<summary>Hint</summary>
	There is a website with profiles of Vietnam Veterans
</details>

<details>
	<summary>Answer</summary>
	Thomas Lee Wade
</details>

### The second challenge
> In which cemetery was he buried?
<details>
	<summary>Hint</summary>
	There is a website where you can search graveyards across the US.
</details>
<details>
	<summary>Answer</summary>
	Mountain View Cemetery
</details>

### The third challenge
> Looking aroudn the graveyard where you found the veteran there are quite a few other plots with a wide array of time between burrials. What is the difference between the oldest recorded plot's death year and the most recently recorded plot's death year? Enter the flag as (most_recent_death_date - oldest_death_date = flag).
<details>
	<summary>Answer</summary>
	1889 minus 2019 is 130.
	Flag is 130.
</details>
